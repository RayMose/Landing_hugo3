How to Earn Points

To earn points and maximize your rewards:

  • Make purchases at any of our participating retailers
  • Use our convenient USSD code for transactions through your mobile phone
  • Invite friends and family to join our Rewards Program
  • Receive extra points when they sign up and make their first purchase

How to Redeem Points

Redeeming your points is a straightforward process designed for your convenience.

  • Use our USSD code to check your points balance and browse our extensive rewards catalog for items or discounts that suit your preferences.
  • Our catalog offers a wide range of options, from grocery discounts to exclusive products.
  • Redeem your points directly through our USSD code, transferring them to your bank account or applying them to your next purchase.
